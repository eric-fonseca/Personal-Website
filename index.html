About

Eric Fonseca is a senior at Rochester Institute of Technology where he is currently majoring in New Media Interactive Development with minors in Advertising and Public Relations as well as Game Design and Development. Over the past two years, he attained co-ops with Excellus BlueCross BlueShield and RIT's Golisano Institute for Sustainability where he pursued his passion for design and front-end web development. Eric spends his free time traveling and volunteering for the Pet Adoption Network where he fosters cats and kittens.
